Number of solutions to a system of equations graphically

Graph the system of equations. \begin{cases} & 2x+y=5 \\\\ & 14x+7y=35 \end{cases} Convert the second equation to slope-intercept form: 

(defn subtract-x [equation]
  (let [left (:l equation)
        right (:r equation)
        x-left (:x (:l equation))
        x-right (:x (:r equation))]
    {:l (dissoc left :x)
     :r (assoc right :x (- x-right x-left))}))

(defn divide-y [equation]
  (let [y-val (:y (:l equation))
        r-keys (keys (:r equation))
        r-vals (vals (:r equation))]
    {:l {:y 1}
     :r (zipmap r-keys
                (map #(/ % y-val) r-vals))}))

(->> {:l {:x 14 :y 7}
      :r {:n 35}}
     subtract-x
     divide-y)
