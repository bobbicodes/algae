Equations with parentheses

Solve for $m$:

$$\Large{3-2(9+2m)=m}$$

Distribute: 

(def equation
  {:left-side
   {:integer 3
    :expression
    {:coefficient -2
     :terms {:integer 9
             :m 2}}}
   :right-side {:m 1}})

(let [expression-left
      (:expression (:left-side equation))
      coefficient-left
      (:coefficient expression-left)
      multiplier
      (:integer (:terms expression-left))]
  [(+ (* coefficient-left multiplier)
     (:integer (:left-side equation)))
   (* coefficient-left
      (:m (:terms expression-left)))])
