Find average speed


Ishaan traveled uphill for 12 minutes at 15 mph. He then traveled back home along the same path downhill at a speed of 30 mph. 


(defn speed [speed-uphill speed-downhill time-uphill]
  (let [distance-uphill (/ (* speed-uphill time-uphill) 60)
        total-distance (* 2 distance-uphill)
        time-downhill (/ (* distance-uphill 60) speed-downhill)
        total-time (+ time-uphill time-downhill)]
    (/ (* 60 total-distance) total-time)))

(speed 15 30 12)
$$\text{average speed} = \dfrac{\text{total distance}}{\text{total time}}$$ $$\text{distance uphill} = \text{distance downhill}$$ $$\begin{aligned} \text{total distance} &= \text{distance uphill} + \text{distance downhill} \\ &= 2 \times \text{distance uphill} \end{aligned}$$ $$\begin{aligned} \text{distance uphill} &= \text{speed uphill} \times \text{time uphill} \\ &= 6\text{ mph} \times 60\text{ minutes} \times \dfrac{1 \text{ hour}}{60 \text{ minutes}}\\ &= 6\text{ miles} \end{aligned}$$ $$\text{total distance} = 12\text{ miles}$$ $$\text{total time} = \text{time uphill} + \text{time downhill}$$ $$\begin{aligned} \text{time downhill} &= \dfrac{\text{distance downhill}}{\text{speed downhill}} \\ &= \dfrac{6\text{ miles}}{12\text{ mph}} \times \dfrac{60 \text{ minutes}}{1 \text{ hour}}\\ &= 30\text{ minutes} \end{aligned}$$ $$\begin{aligned} \text{total time} &= 60\text{ minutes} + 30\text{ minutes} \\ &= 90\text{ minutes} \end{aligned}$$ $$\begin{aligned} \text{average speed} &= \dfrac{\text{total distance}}{\text{total time}} \\ &= \dfrac{12\text{ miles}} {90\text{ minutes}} \times \dfrac{60 \text{ minutes}}{1 \text{ hour}} \\ &= 8\text{ mph} \end{aligned}$$

Rate problems 2

It takes 36 minutes for 7 people to paint 4 walls.

How many minutes does it take 9 people to paint 7 walls?

First calculate how long it will take one person to paint one wall. 

(defn paint [minutes people walls]
  (* people (/ minutes walls)))

(paint 36 7 4)
Number of minutes for one person to paint 7 walls: 

(defn walls [per-wall people walls]
  (/ (* walls per-wall) people))

(walls 63 9 7)
