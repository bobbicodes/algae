Slope from graph

Going from the point on the left to the point on the right, the change in y is 2, and the change in x is 3. $$\text{Slope}=\dfrac{\text{rise}}{\text{run}}=\dfrac{\Delta y}{\Delta x}=\dfrac23$$ The slope of the line is: $$\dfrac{2}{3}$$

$$2x+5y=-6$$ 

(defn round [precision d]
  (let [factor (Math/pow 10 precision)]
    (/ (Math/round (* d factor)) factor)))

; find y intercept:

(loop [x 0 y -100]
  (if (= (+ (* 2 x)
            (* 5 y))
         -6)
      y
 (recur x
   (round 1 (+ y 0.1)))))
Find x intercept: 

(loop [x -100 y 0]
  (if (= (+ (* 2 x)
            (* 5 y))
         -6)
      x
      (recur (round 1 (+ x 0.1))
             y)))
